Company overview

KSW India Private Limited, formerly Krishna Scientific Works established by Mr. Rajender Singh in the Year 1989, with the desire to manufacture Surface Finishing Plant at the time of emerging needs in the surface Finishing requirements, with a Vision to be a “Leader among the World's Best Surface Finishing Technology Providers, offering each of our clients in Customized, Safe and Environment-friendly solution in International Standards on Turn Key Basis”.

From the day, the company has been started the company works with the Mission as “We are committed to provide complete surface finishing solutions with innovative designs, incorporating latest value engineered technology at affordable cost with systematic and on-going customer support to ensure our clients enhanced productivity and profitability”.

Quality policy, as “KSW will supply products and Solutions that will meet the Customer’s Expectations without Exception. We are Committed to identify Opportunities for continuous improvement to our process, Product & People every time”.

At present, we are actively supporting industries in India and abroad through its no of Service Locations for setting up of Surface Finishing plants on turn key basis.

The management comprises a dedicated team of professionals, having experience of more than 20 years, across the surface coating spectrum. They are ably supported by trained personnel with in-house expertise in Designing, Manufacturing and Installation of all aspects of Surface Finishing plants. The 135 - member Production and Installation team is fully equipped for offering time-bound solutions to customers.

In addition, the main factory shop floor having 8000 Sq. Mtrs of Space & with a Constructed area of 5000 Sq. Mtrs, has its own modern Machineries and Facility for Production, Testing and Installation of high quality Surface Finishing solutions to our esteemed clients.

Over two and half decades, our growing clientele of satisfied OEMs and their vendor companies has established us as the most trustworthy name, in this field with a title of “Comprehensive Surface Finishing Solutions, Since 1989” In India and Abroad.
